Frequently asked user functionality questions

Once logged in, go to your overview tab. Here, you will be able to search for tracks by inputting both the track name, artist name, and clicking search. Then, just click 'add' on the search result you desire, and that song will be added to your favorites.
Once you have added at least 5 songs to your favorites, click on the Spotify Soulmates tab. Here, other user profiles will be displayed that match your musical tastes! Through this page, you can obtain their contact information and reach out to them.
Spotify Soulmate searchs for possible Soulmates using our matching algorithms. These algorithms take into account your favorite tracks, artists, and genres to find you the best possible matches to your musical tastes.

Frequently asked error questions

While the Spotify API gives us access to tens of millions of songs, not every song is on Spotify. Try double checking your search terms and try again. If you feel like recieving no results may be an error, please contact the Spotify Soulmate team, your help is grealty appreciated!
Don't worry, it is unlikely that nobody shares your musical tastes. Spotify Soulmate is a growing service with an expanding user base. Check back in a few days, and likely someone with similar tastes will have joined by them.
Spotify Soulmate is a growing service built by a small team of developers. In such a small and agile team, bugs are unavoidable. Please reach out to the Spotify Soulmate team to report your specific bug, your help is greatly appreciated.
